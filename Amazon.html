About International Products

International products are from outside the United States and may differ from your local versions, including in their fit, default settings, age ratings, and language. Any manufacturer warranties on international products may not apply. Any plugs or adaptors may not be compatible with the standards in your shipping destination. Certain products, such as software and DVDs, may be region locked against use in your shipping destination. Product packaging, labels, and instructions may also be in a language other than U.S. English. You should carefully review all available product information to ensure that the international product you are purchasing meets your needs and expectations.

We will charge you in U.S. dollars, but you may receive an invoice with your delivery showing the cost in a different currency.

When you buy international products from outside the United States, Amazon Global Store EU Sarl is the seller and you are the importer of record and must pay import duties, taxes, and fees when the products arrive in your shipping destination. Amazon Global Store simplifies this process by estimating the amount that will be due (the “Import Fees Deposit”) and adding it to the amount you pay. You authorize international carriers working with Amazon to pay this amount to local authorities on your (or the recipient’s) behalf. If Amazon’s estimate for the Import Fees Deposit is greater than what is needed, you will be automatically refunded the difference to your original payment method . You will never be asked to pay more than the estimated Import Fees Deposit.
